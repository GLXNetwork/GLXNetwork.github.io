About us

GLX is a fortnite discord server made to make it easier to find people to play with! GLX was founded back in april 2019. We have an awsome and active community with over 400 members and growing everyday! We have channels for boxfights, zone wars and much more to get you the BEST practise and for ALL regions! We take Wager Scams very seriously and we do everything we can to reduce wager scams as much as possible! For this reasone we have made a 100% scammer free wager system! More info will be found inside the server. We also host our own insanly stacked zone wars everyday!
